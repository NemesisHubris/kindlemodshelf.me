KWordle – Wordle for Kindle

KWordle brings the classic Wordle word-guessing game to your Kindle e-reader. Built specifically for e-ink screens, KWordle offers the same addictive gameplay with a Kindle-friendly design and can be played completely offline after installation.
Overview
KWordle is a Kindle-optimized port of the popular Wordle game. Guess a hidden 5-letter word within six attempts, using helpful visual indicators that are easy to see on black-and-white Kindle displays. The game tracks your statistics and works in multiple languages, so you can play and improve your vocabulary in English, French, German, and more.
Features
  • Classic Wordle gameplay: Guess the 5-letter word in six tries
  • E-ink optimized visuals:
    ■ Filled square: Letter is correct and in the right position
    □ Empty square: Letter is correct but in the wrong position
    × Cross: Letter is not in the word
  • On-screen keyboard with feedback for guessed letters
  • Play in multiple languages (English, French, German; more can be added)
  • Statistics: Tracks win percentage and streaks
  • Works completely offline—no internet needed after install
  • Compatible with Kindle’s WebKit browser and all major Kindle models
How to Install
  1. Download the latest KWordle release from GitHub: github.com/crizmo/KWordle
  2. Connect your Kindle to your computer via USB.
  3. Copy the entire kwordle folder and kwordle.sh script to your Kindle’s documents folder.
  4. Disconnect your Kindle from your computer.
  5. On your Kindle, open the KWordle booklet or run the kwordle.sh script to launch the game.
How to Play
  1. The game picks a random 5-letter word.
  2. Type in your guess using the on-screen or Kindle keyboard and press “Enter.”
  3. After each guess, you’ll see visual indicators:
    ■ Letter is in the correct spot
    □ Letter is in the word but in the wrong place
    × Letter is not in the word
  4. You have six attempts to find the correct word.
  5. Try to win in as few guesses as possible!
Game Statistics
KWordle tracks your win percentage and streaks, storing your results right on your Kindle (localStorage).
Language Support & Customization
KWordle includes word lists for English, French, and German.

To add a new language:
  1. Create two lists: one for words to guess, one for all valid five-letter words.
  2. Place these lists in the words.js file.
  3. Add your language to the language selection in main.js.
  4. Replace accented letters with unaccented equivalents.
  5. Credit the source of your word lists in the app’s credits section.
Technical Details
  • Uses ES5 JavaScript for compatibility with older Kindle models
  • Optimized for e-ink and smaller screens
  • Works fully offline after installation
  • No known issues reported as of the latest release
