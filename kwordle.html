KWordle – Wordle for Kindle

KWordle brings the classic Wordle puzzle game to your Kindle e-reader. Built for e-ink, KWordle is offline, multilingual, and offers simple visuals for black-and-white screens.

About KWordle

Guess the hidden 5-letter word in six attempts. Use on-screen feedback designed for Kindle:
■ = correct letter and spot, □ = correct letter wrong spot, × = not in the word.
Track your win streak and stats. Play in English, French, German, and more. No ads or tracking.

Features

  • Classic Wordle gameplay—guess a 5-letter word in 6 tries
  • Visual feedback for every guess (optimized for e-ink)
  • Multilingual: English, French, German, customizable word lists
  • Statistics: Win percentage and streaks saved locally
  • Works fully offline—no internet required after install
  • Compatible with Kindle’s WebKit browser and all major models

Installation

  1. Download the latest release from KWordle on GitHub.
  2. Connect your Kindle by USB and copy the kwordle folder and kwordle.sh script to your documents folder.
  3. Disconnect, then open the KWordle booklet or run kwordle.sh to launch the game.

How to Play

  1. Game picks a random 5-letter word.
  2. Enter guesses with the on-screen or Kindle keyboard.
  3. After each guess: ■ = correct spot, □ = wrong spot, × = not in word
  4. You have 6 tries. See if you can guess the word!

Language Support & Customization

KWordle supports multiple languages.
To add a new language:
  1. Create lists of guess and valid words, add to words.js.
  2. Update the language selection in main.js.
  3. Replace accented with unaccented letters.
  4. Credit your sources in the credits.

Technical Details

  • Uses ES5 JavaScript for broad Kindle compatibility
  • Optimized for e-ink and small screens
  • All game data stored locally on your Kindle
Enjoy Wordle anywhere—right on your Kindle, ad-free and offline.
