Disable Ads % – Enable Custom Screensavers on Kindle

Disable Ads % is a simple tool for jailbroken, ad-supported Kindles. Instantly enable KOReader custom screensavers—including book cover images and your own pictures—even on Kindles with ads.
What Does It Do?
Disable Ads % enables KOReader custom screensavers on ad-supported Kindles. Use this script if you want book cover screensavers or personal images on your Kindle’s lock screen with KOReader.
Features
  • Enables KOReader custom screensaver and book cover features on ad-supported Kindles
  • One-click install—no advanced skills or manual file editing needed
  • Works on most jailbroken ad-supported Kindle models
Requirements
  • Jailbroken Kindle e-reader with Special Offers (ad-supported)
  • KOReader (for custom screensaver/book cover support)
How to Use
  1. Download the script: disable_ads.sh
  2. Copy to Kindle: Place disable_ads.sh in your Kindle’s documents folder.
  3. Run the script: On your Kindle, open your library and tap disable_ads.sh to execute it.
  4. Restart your Kindle. Custom screensaver features will now be enabled.
Notes
  • This script is required for KOReader custom screensaver and book cover features on ad-supported Kindles.
  • Tested on all major jailbroken Kindle models with ads.
