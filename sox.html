SOX Media Player for Kindle

SOX Media Player lets you play music, audiobooks, and internet streams over Bluetooth on your jailbroken Kindle. Supports popular audio formats including WAV, MP3, FLAC, OGG, and more. Softfloat (SF) and hardfloat (HF) versions are available.
Get the latest SOX Media Player (SF & HF) – Download & instructions on MobileRead

Features

  • Bluetooth audio playback for music, audiobooks, and internet streams
  • Supports WAV, MP3, FLAC, OGG, AIFF, AU, GSM, RAW, CAF, VOC, W64, WVE, XA, XI, and more
  • Playlist support: M3U, PLS
  • Simple KUAL-based interface
  • Custom internet radio/streams support (edit menu.json)

Requirements

  • Jailbroken Kindle with KUAL and Bluetooth support
  • Download either SF (softfloat) or HF (hardfloat) version, matching your Kindle firmware
  • Music or audiobooks in /mnt/us/music (the Kindle’s music folder)

How to Install

  1. Visit the official SOX Media Player thread and download the correct zip for your firmware (SF or HF).
  2. Unzip on your computer.
  3. Copy the SOX folder to extensions/ on your Kindle.
  4. Put your music or audiobooks in /mnt/us/music on your Kindle.
  5. Safely eject and disconnect your Kindle.

How to Use

  1. Pair your Bluetooth audio device with your Kindle in Settings.
  2. Open KUAL on your Kindle.
  3. Launch SOX Media Player from the KUAL menu.
  4. For internet radio, edit menu.json to add streams (see the README and thread for setup).

Notes & Troubleshooting

  • All files in one playlist/call must have the same format, rate, channels, and bit depth.
  • SOX expects files in the music directory. Custom menu items can be added in menu.json.
  • Bluetooth can sometimes be unreliable; if playback freezes, reconnect your device or restart Kindle.
  • AAC files are not supported. Any attempt to play AAC will fail.
