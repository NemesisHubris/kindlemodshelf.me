Tetris for KTerm (HF Only)

Play classic Tetris on your jailbroken Kindle, right in the terminal.
Tetris for KTerm is a lightweight Tetris game for Kindles running hardfloat (HF) firmware—no emulator needed.

Requirements
  • Jailbroken Kindle with hardfloat (HF) firmware
  • KTerm terminal installed (see KTerm page)
How to Install

  1. Download the game:
    Download kindletetris.zip

  2. Unzip the file on your computer.

  3. Copy the extracted files to your Kindle. Place them in your preferred directory (such as /extensions).

  4. Eject and disconnect your Kindle from your computer.

How to Play

  1. Open KTerm on your Kindle.

  2. Navigate to the directory where you put the Tetris files (using cd command).

  3. Start the game by typing:
    ./kindletetris

  4. Play Tetris in the terminal window!

Notes
  • This version works only on hardfloat (HF) firmware models. It is not compatible with softfloat (SF) devices.
  • You must launch Tetris from inside KTerm; it is not a standalone app or menu item.
  • Want Game Boy Tetris? Use a Game Boy emulator instead.

Enjoy classic Tetris—right from your Kindle’s terminal!
