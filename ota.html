Check OTA Status – Kindle Update Safety Tool

Check OTA Status is a quick, read-only script that checks whether Over-The-Air (OTA) updates are blocked on your Kindle. Instantly see if your device is protected from unwanted automatic updates and keep your jailbreak safe.

Key Features

  • Instant OTA Checker: See at a glance whether your Kindle will auto-update or is protected.
  • Persistent Status Display: Status message stays on screen until you tap to exit.
  • Safe & Read-Only: Does not modify your device—purely informational.

Why Use Check OTA Status?

  • Block Kindle Updates: Know for sure if OTA updates are blocked—critical for anyone with a jailbroken Kindle.
  • Peace of Mind: Prevent accidental updates and protect your jailbreak from being lost.

Example Status Messages

  • OTA blocking is enabled: Your Kindle will NOT update. Your jailbreak is safe.
  • OTA blocking is disabled: Your Kindle can be updated. Your jailbreak is in danger.

Tap the screen to exit.

Compatibility

  • Tested on Kindle KT4 and recent models
  • Works on most jailbroken Kindles
