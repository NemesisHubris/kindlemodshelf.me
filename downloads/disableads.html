Disable Ads % – Remove Kindle Ads & Enable Custom Screensavers

Disable Ads % is a tool for jailbroken, ad-supported Kindles that removes Amazon ads and unlocks the ability to use custom screensavers with KOReader—including book covers.
What Does It Do?
This script disables the ads (Special Offers) on your Kindle and enables support for custom screensavers. It is required if you want KOReader to display book covers or your own images as screensavers on an ad-supported Kindle.
Features
  • Removes Amazon ads from ad-supported Kindles (Special Offers models)
  • Enables KOReader's custom screensaver feature (including book cover support)
  • Works instantly—no manual file editing required
  • Can be easily reversed by restoring your original files (if needed)
Requirements
  • Jailbroken Kindle e-reader (ad-supported/Special Offers model)
    (How to jailbreak your Kindle)
  • KTerm terminal emulator or a way to run shell scripts
  • KOReader (required to use custom screensavers or book covers)
How to Use
  1. Download the script: disable_ads.sh
  2. Copy disable_ads.sh to your Kindle (for example, inside /mnt/us/extensions/ or your preferred folder)
  3. Open KTerm on your Kindle
  4. Navigate to the script location with cd
  5. Run the script:
    sh disable_ads.sh
  6. Restart your Kindle. Ads should be gone and custom screensavers enabled.
Notes
  • This tool is required to use KOReader's custom screensaver feature if your Kindle is ad-supported.
  • Enables book cover display as screensaver (with KOReader).
  • Tested on most modern jailbroken Kindle models with ads.
  • To undo changes, restore your original ad-related files or re-enable ads manually.
