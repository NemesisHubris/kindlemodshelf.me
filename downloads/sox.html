SOX Media Player (HF, Beta) for Kindle

SOX Media Player lets you play WAV audio files on your Kindle over Bluetooth. This is a dedicated port for hardfloat (HF) firmware Kindles and is currently in beta—some features may still be experimental or unstable.
Features
  • Play WAV audio files on your Kindle
  • Bluetooth audio support: Listen with Bluetooth headphones or speakers
  • Simple, no-frills interface
  • Beta status: Player is under active development; not all features are fully stable yet
Requirements
  • Jailbroken Kindle with hardfloat (HF) firmware
  • Bluetooth support on your Kindle
  • Your audio files in WAV format
How to Install
  1. Download the player:
    Download soxhf.zip
  2. Unzip the file on your computer.
  3. Copy the files to your Kindle (choose a directory you can find easily).
  4. Eject and disconnect your Kindle.
How to Use
  1. Open your preferred terminal or file manager on Kindle.
  2. Launch the SOX player from the directory where you copied the files.
  3. Connect your Bluetooth audio device.
  4. Start playing your WAV files!
Notes
  • HF firmware only: This player only works on Kindles running hardfloat firmware.
  • Still in beta: Some features may be incomplete or unstable.
