Z-Library Plugin for KOReader

The Z-Library KOReader Plugin makes it easy to search for and download books from Z-Library right inside KOReader—no need to exit the app or use a separate device.
Features
  • Search and download Z-Library books directly from KOReader
  • Clean, integrated interface—no need to exit KOReader
  • Supports both free and paid Z-Library accounts
  • Paid Z-Library users: download over 10 books per day
  • Free Z-Library users: limited to 10 downloads per day
How to Set Up
  1. Download the repository as a ZIP: Download KOReader Z-Library Plugin (GitHub)
  2. Unpack the ZIP file.
  3. Copy the unpacked folder into your Kindle at koreader/plugins.
  4. Restart KOReader if it's running.
  5. Log in on your Kindle:
    Open KOReader, go to the Search section, and enter your Z-Library account details.
Usage Notes
  • Use the Search in KOReader to browse and download books directly from Z-Library.
  • If you have a paid Z-Library account, you can download more than 10 books per day.
  • Free accounts are limited to 10 downloads per day.
Important Safety and Privacy Notice
Always use the real Z-Library website:
Only trust the current, official Z-Library link—check their official X account for updates.
Fake Z-Library sites may contain malware or spyware.

Privacy:
You will log in to Z-Library from your Kindle. Kindles do not have built-in VPN or advanced privacy features—your activity can be tracked.
Summary
The Z-Library Plugin for KOReader gives you a simple, in-app way to expand your Kindle library, with a smooth interface and support for both free and paid Z-Library accounts.
Make sure you use the official site and keep your account safe.
