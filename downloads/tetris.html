Tetris for KTerm (HF Only)

Tetris for KTerm brings classic Tetris gameplay to your Kindle, right in the terminal.
It’s a lightweight Tetris game you can play directly on hardfloat (HF) firmware Kindles—no emulator needed.
Requirements
  • Jailbroken Kindle with hardfloat (HF) firmware
  • KTerm terminal installed and set up (see KTerm page)
How to Install
  1. Download the game:
    Download kindletetris.zip
  2. Unzip the file on your computer.
  3. Copy the extracted files to your Kindle—place them in your preferred directory (such as inside your /extensions folder).
  4. Eject and disconnect your Kindle from your computer.
How to Play
  1. Open KTerm on your Kindle.
  2. Navigate to the directory where you put the Tetris files (using cd command).
  3. Run the game by typing:
    ./kindletetris
  4. Play Tetris in the terminal window!
Notes
  • Works only on HF firmware models (not compatible with softfloat/SF devices).
  • Must be launched from inside KTerm—this is not a standalone app or menu item.
  • For Game Boy Tetris, use a Game Boy emulator as an alternative.
Enjoy classic Tetris, right from your Kindle’s terminal!
