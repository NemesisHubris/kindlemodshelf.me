Check OTA Status – Kindle Update Safety Tool

Check OTA Status is a simple tool for Kindle that lets you instantly check whether Over-The-Air (OTA) updates are enabled or blocked on your device.

It does not change any settings or files—it just tells you the current OTA status so you can keep your jailbreak safe.
Key Features
  • Instant OTA Status: Clearly shows whether your Kindle is safe from automatic updates or not.
  • Persistent Display: Status message remains on screen until you tap to exit.
  • No Changes Made: This scriptlet is purely informational and will not modify your system.
Why Use This?
  • Essential for Jailbreakers: See at a glance if your device is at risk of unwanted updates.
  • Stay Informed: Helps you monitor update status so you don’t accidentally lose your jailbreak.
Example Status Messages
  • OTA blocking is enabled.
    Your Kindle will NOT update. Your jailbreak is safe.
  • OTA blocking is disabled.
    Your Kindle can be updated. Your jailbreak is in danger.

Just tap the screen to exit when you’re done.

Compatibility
  • Tested on: Kindle KT4
  • Should work on most jailbroken Kindles
