KOReader – A Better Kindle Reading Experience

KOReader is a powerful, free reading app for jailbroken Kindles. It opens many more file types than Amazon’s built-in reader and gives you more control over how your books look.
Main Features
  • Supports all major formats: PDF, EPUB, DJVU, CBZ, MOBI, DOC, RTF, TXT, and more
  • Fast and responsive: Quicker page turns and loading than the default Kindle reader
  • Customizable: Change margins, font size, line spacing, contrast, and more
  • Dictionary and translation: Look up words or translate instantly while reading
  • Extra tools: Send books wirelessly, zoom into comics, add screensavers, and more
  • Designed for e-ink: No unnecessary animations, just clear pages and easy reading
Why Use KOReader?
  • Read any ebook or document
  • Make your Kindle look and work how you want
  • Enjoy features not possible with the standard Kindle app
How to Install KOReader
  1. Jailbreak your Kindle
    (Required to run apps like KOReader)
  2. Download KOReader for Kindle: KOReader Latest Release
  3. Unzip and copy the files to your Kindle
    (Follow the instructions included with KOReader)
  4. Launch KOReader
    Open it using KUAL (Kindle Unified Application Launcher) or from your Kindle’s home screen.
  5. For step-by-step help: See the KOReader Installation Guide
Summary
KOReader makes your Kindle a faster, more flexible, and more powerful reading device.
If you want to read more formats, customize your experience, and get the most out of your Kindle, KOReader is the way to go.
