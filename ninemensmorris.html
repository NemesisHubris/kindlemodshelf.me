Nine Men’s Morris for Kindle – KUAL & KTerm Versions

Enjoy Nine Men’s Morris, the classic strategy board game, on your Kindle. Choose the version for your device: Softfloat (SF) for KUAL menu launch, or Hardfloat (HF) for command-line play in KTerm.
Download Nine Men’s Morris
  • Softfloat (SF) Version (KUAL App): Download from MobileRead
    Launch directly from the KUAL menu.
  • Hardfloat (HF) Version (KTerm Only): Download morris-kindle.zip
    Launch via command line in KTerm (see below).
Requirements
  • For SF: Jailbroken Kindle with softfloat firmware, KUAL installed
  • For HF: Jailbroken Kindle with hardfloat firmware, KTerm terminal emulator
How to Install
  • For SF: Follow instructions in the MobileRead post to install and launch from KUAL.
  • For HF: Unzip the download, copy files to your Kindle, and follow the instructions below to launch from KTerm.
How to Play (HF Version)
  1. Open KTerm on your Kindle.
  2. Navigate to the directory where you placed the game (use cd command).
  3. Run:
    ./morris-kindle
  4. Play Nine Men’s Morris in your terminal window.
Notes
  • SF version: Use KUAL for a simple menu-driven experience.
  • Classic strategy, easy setup, works offline. Enjoy timeless board gaming on your Kindle!
