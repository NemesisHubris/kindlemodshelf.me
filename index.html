Welcome to KindleModShelf
A curated resource hub for Kindle modding and customization.

KindleModShelf is an independent online resource for Kindle device modding.

Our goal is to provide a single, organized place for discovering open-source Kindle tools, guides, and plugins created by the community. This site is strictly a resource and reference page. We do not sell products or services.

  • Access a curated collection of Kindle modding tools and utilities
  • Find links to official projects, guides, and documentation
  • Learn more about customizing and enhancing your Kindle
  • All resources are credited to their original authors and projects

If you have questions about the resources or would like your project included, we are happy to help.
Please contact us at admin@kindlemodshelf.me.

About This Website

KindleModShelf is a volunteer-maintained collection of links and information for the Kindle modding community. We do not create or distribute original software, nor do we offer any paid products or services. All content is provided as a convenience to Kindle users interested in learning about device modification.
