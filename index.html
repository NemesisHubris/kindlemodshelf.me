Kindle Modding Tools & Resources

The most essential Kindle modding tools, plugins, games, and guides for your jailbroken Kindle. Everything here requires a jailbroken Kindle unless stated otherwise.

Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need to jailbreak my Kindle to use these tools?
Yes, unless stated otherwise.
Is jailbreaking safe?
If you follow the guide, yes. You can always reset if needed.
How do I jailbreak my Kindle?
See our jailbreak guide for steps.
Can I downgrade my Kindle to jailbreak?
No, you must jailbreak first before downgrading.
Will I lose my books or highlights?
No, jailbreaking doesn’t delete your books or highlights.
Can I undo the jailbreak?
Yes. Factory reset your Kindle to remove jailbreak.
Will jailbreaking void my warranty?
Probably.
Will my Amazon account get banned?
No known cases, but don’t tell support.
Can I still use the Amazon store and Kindle services?
Yes, everything works as usual.
Can I use KOReader to read Amazon ebooks?
No, KOReader doesn’t support Kindle’s DRM formats.
Where can I get more help?
Read the full FAQ and Guide.
Most Popular & Essential Tools
KOReader
Essential
Powerful open-source document reader for Kindle supporting EPUB, PDF, DjVu, CBZ/CBR, and more. Adds features like custom screensavers, USB/network transfer, Bluetooth audio, plugins, dictionary, theming, and advanced file management.
Learn more
KindleFetch
Essential
Command-line tool to download books from Anna’s Archive directly to your Kindle using the built-in terminal. Requires KTerm terminal.
Learn more
KTerm (Kindle Terminal Emulator)
Essential
Terminal emulator for Kindle. Access a command-line interface for running scripts, mods, and tools directly on your device.
