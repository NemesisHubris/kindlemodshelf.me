Gnome Games for Kindle: Chess & Minesweeper

Gnome Chess and Gnome Mines bring classic strategy and puzzle gameplay to your Kindle. These games are direct ports of the beloved Gnome Games suite (version 2.91.93) and are designed to run smoothly on jailbroken Kindle e-readers, with no extra dependencies.
What’s Included
  • Gnome Chess: Play chess against the computer, powered by the integrated Stockfish chess engine.
  • Gnome Mines: The classic Minesweeper puzzle game, fully playable with Kindle touch controls.
Key Features
  • Kindle-Optimized: Built specifically for armel (softfloat) and armhf (hardfloat) Kindle models. Tested on firmware 5.14.3 and 5.17.1.
  • No extra libraries required: Everything runs with pre-installed GTK2 on your Kindle—just extract and play.
  • Fast and responsive: Enjoy smooth gameplay and reliable performance on Kindle’s hardware.
  • Easy access: Launch the games from KUAL or add shortcuts to your Kindle home screen.
How to Install
  1. Download the latest gnomegames.zip: Direct download link
  2. Connect your Kindle to your computer via USB.
  3. Unpack the ZIP to /mnt/us/extensions/ on your Kindle.
  4. Open KUAL (Kindle Unified Application Launcher):
    You’ll find Gnome Chess and Gnome Mines listed as extensions.
  5. (Optional) Copy the included shortcut scripts (e.g., shortcut_gnomechess.sh) to /mnt/us/documents to show the games in your Kindle library.
How to Play
  1. Launch either Gnome Chess or Gnome Mines from KUAL or your Kindle’s documents.
  2. Use the touchscreen controls to play.
  3. Chess features the Stockfish engine for a challenging AI opponent.
  4. Minesweeper offers the classic grid puzzle with intuitive touch support.
Technical Notes
  • These games are direct GTK2 ports and do not require any external libraries.
  • Compatible with both hardfloat and softfloat firmware (armhf and armel).
  • Designed for Kindle models running 5.14.3, 5.17.1, or similar.
  • Fully offline—no internet required after installation.
