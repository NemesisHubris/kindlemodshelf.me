Gnome Games for Kindle: Chess & Minesweeper

Gnome Chess and Gnome Mines bring classic chess and Minesweeper gameplay to your Kindle. Direct ports of the Gnome Games suite, built for Kindle e-readers with smooth, responsive touch controls.
What’s Included
  • Gnome Chess: Challenge the integrated Stockfish chess engine right on your Kindle.
  • Gnome Mines: Play classic Minesweeper with touch controls and fast response.
Key Features
  • Optimized for Kindle (armel and armhf; softfloat and hardfloat)
  • No extra libraries required—built with pre-installed GTK2
  • Fast gameplay and reliable performance on all supported firmware
  • Launch directly from KUAL (Kindle Unified Application Launcher)
  • Works fully offline—no internet required after install
How to Install
  1. Download gnomegames.zip: Direct download link
  2. Connect your Kindle to your computer by USB.
  3. Unpack the ZIP to /mnt/us/extensions/ on your Kindle.
  4. Open KUAL—Gnome Chess and Gnome Mines will appear as extensions.
How to Play
  1. Launch Gnome Chess or Gnome Mines from KUAL.
  2. Use touch controls to play each game.
  3. Chess uses Stockfish for a strong computer opponent; Minesweeper offers the classic puzzle grid.
Technical Notes
  • Direct GTK2 ports—no extra dependencies needed.
  • Compatible with softfloat and hardfloat firmware (armel/armhf).
  • Tested on Kindle firmware 5.14.3 and 5.17.1.
  • Fully offline after setup.
