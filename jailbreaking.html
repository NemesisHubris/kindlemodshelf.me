Jailbreaking Your Kindle

Jailbreaking unlocks your Kindle’s true potential, letting you install custom apps, tools, and features that Amazon doesn’t support. If you want to run KOReader, custom screensavers, emulators, or other mods, you’ll need to jailbreak your Kindle first.
